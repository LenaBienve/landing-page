SYNOPSIS

The series follows the adventures of Léna, a fearless space adventurer, who travels the universe in the distant future. Dressed in her silver suit and armed with a blaster, she is a force to be reckoned with. Each volume sees her facing challenges that push her to her limits and make her grow as a guardian of the galaxy.
